    Defining the Problem

            

    Resource Scarcity

    Muslim refugees worldwide face significant challenges in accessing basic necessities, including food, shelter, medical support, and education.

    Limited Support Channels

    Insufficient resources and limited channels exist to support these communities in a transparent and accountable way.

    Need for Transparency

    There is a lack of dedicated, secure platforms for donors who want to support refugees while ensuring their contributions are fully traceable.

    Website to Support Muslim Refugees

    In response to the ongoing global refugee crisis, a new mobile app has been launched to connect donors directly with Muslim refugees in need. This innovative platform offers a seamless, transparent, and culturally sensitive way for donors to contribute to essential causes such as food, clothing, medical aid, and infrastructure.

    The app provides a user-friendly experience, allowing donors to select specific causes and contribute with ease. It offers real-time updates, ensuring transparency and showing the tangible impact of each donation. By integrating cultural elements like Zakat and Sadaqah, the app aligns with Islamic charitable practices, making it easier for Muslim donors to give according to their faith.

    This initiative aims to foster trust and empower both refugees and donors, ensuring that aid reaches those who need it most, while providing a sense of community and solidarity in the face of the refugee crisis.

    Final Product Features

    The finished app offers a streamlined experience where donors can quickly see ongoing projects, make targeted donations, and receive detailed updates on the impact of their contributions. It emphasizes transparency, security, and cultural sensitivity.
